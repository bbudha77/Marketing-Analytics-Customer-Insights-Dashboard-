# 📊 Marketing Analytics & Customer Insights Dashboard

## 📌 Business Problem

ShopEasy, an online retail company, experienced **declining customer engagement and decreasing conversion rates** despite investing heavily in online marketing campaigns. At the same time, marketing expenses continued to rise while the return on investment remained lower than expected.

The company needed a **data-driven analysis** to understand:

- Why conversion rates were dropping
- Why customer engagement was decreasing
- How customer feedback and product performance influence purchasing behaviour

The objective was to identify key issues in the marketing funnel and provide insights to help improve marketing performance and customer experience.



# 🛠 What I Did

To address the problem, I conducted **data analysis using SQL** and built an **interactive Power BI dashboard** to visualize marketing performance.

### Data Analysis (SQL)

I used SQL to:

- Clean and structure the marketing dataset
- Calculate important KPIs such as:
  - Conversion Rate
  - Views, Clicks and Likes
  - Average Customer Rating
- Analyze trends across:
  - Months
  - Product categories
  - Customer engagement metrics

SQL helped transform raw data into structured insights that could be used for visualization.

---

### Dashboard Development (Power BI)

After preparing the data, I developed an **interactive Power BI dashboard** that provides insights into marketing performance.

The dashboard includes:

- Conversion Rate analysis by **month and product**
- Social media engagement metrics (**views, clicks, likes**)
- Customer rating analysis
- Product performance comparison

Interactive filters allow users to explore the data by:

- Product category
- Year
- Month

---

# 📈 Key Insights

### Conversion Rate Trends
Conversion rates fluctuated across the year. Some months performed significantly better than others, suggesting **seasonal purchasing behaviour** and opportunities for targeted marketing campaigns.

### Customer Engagement
Customer engagement showed a **declining trend in views and interactions**, indicating that marketing content may need improvement to maintain audience interest.

Although views were high, the number of clicks and likes remained lower, suggesting opportunities to improve **call-to-action strategies and content quality**.

### Customer Feedback
Customer ratings remained relatively stable around **3.7 out of 5**, indicating generally positive feedback but still below the target of **4.0**. Improving product quality and customer service could help increase customer satisfaction and conversions.

---

# 💡 Solution & Business Value

The dashboard provides actionable insights that help the marketing team:

- Identify **high-performing products**
- Detect **months with low conversion rates**
- Understand **customer engagement patterns**
- Improve **marketing campaign effectiveness**

By using these insights, the company can optimize marketing strategies, improve customer satisfaction, and ultimately **increase conversion rates and sales performance**.
